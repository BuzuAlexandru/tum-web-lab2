About us

Although agriculture is the main field of activity of the entire population of the Republic of Moldova and has great potential in our country, this sector lacks qualified specialists and innovative technologies. As a university of excellence in the field of research and innovation, UTM keeps in step with the new trends in the field of digitalization and continues to revolutionize the agricultural field. Thus, the Technical University of Moldova, in the person of the rector, Prof. Viorel BOSTAN, Dr. Habil., in partnership with the Micro Lab Engineering Club, launched the “Agro IT Arena” project, the purpose of which is to bring innovative solutions to agriculture.

Projects


Agrobot



A ground based drone designed for agricultural work. Its potential limitless, from planting and irigation to pest control and harvesting, the rover can be built to do it all. Based on cutting-edge GPS technology, it will leave in the spring and return in the autumn performing all work autonomously.

Swarm drones



A cluster of aerial drones designated to scan growing plants to then be able to perform predictive analysis to figure out the amount of harvest one would get from their fruit trees. That information can then be used to reduce growth in some areas in order to obtain the same harvest consistently.

Smart greenhouse



Called “Smart Environment”, involves the creation of an environment with controlled artificial climate, fully automated and completely excluding human involvement in the growth of agricultural crops

Sorting robot arm



A CNC machine equiped with a camera, capable of distinguishing objects with a high precision and sorting them. Objects are picked up by pulling a vacuum at the end of the mechanic appendage.
